Town management for the modern world.

What is Populace?

Populace is a town management plugin that works with MelonEco. I felt as though Towny was not only outdated, but difficult for new users to grasp, and thus Populace was born.

What's different?

Populace is driven mostly by a powerful ingame GUI. While commands are used for simple functions, most of the information and interaction is done through the interface.

What does it do?

As of now, Populace can:

  • Allow your players to create towns, claim land, delegate permissions, and invite residents
  • Provides a great anti-grief solution that protects against all known forms of griefing
  • Creates thriving economies in survival servers
  • Allows creation of unique shops using Ender Chests and an ingame UI (Requires PopulaceMarket)
  • Allows establishment of sales tax to bring in more income for town mayors (Requires PopulaceMarket)
  • Includes a powerful GUI for viewing town information and making management of complex tasks easy
  • Includes simple commands resulting in a very small learning curve for new players
  • Provides a complex and intuitive chat channel system, including town specific chat (Requires PopulaceChat)
  • Includes an in-depth API (including events and direct hooking to Populace inner workings) for 3rd party developers
  • Cleans up chat by only showing join and quit messages to fellow town members (Requires PopulaceJoin)
  • Adds town rank and town to player names in TAB and above heads. (Requires PopulaceScoreboards)
  • Allows selling of town plots to residents
  • Jail system to allow town staff to punish naughty residents, while allowing the jailed individual to leave early by choosing to leave town
  • Much more...

Town Leveling

As towns gain residents, they level up! THe higher the town level, the more land that can be claimed, and the more features that players unlock.

Level Residents Required Upkeep Modifier Max Land
Settlement 1 1.0x 16 Chunks
Hamlet 2 1.0x 32 Chunks
Village 6 1.0x 96 Chunks
Town 10 1.0x 160 Chunks
Large Town 15 1.1x 224 Chunks
City 25 1.2x 320 Chunks
Large City 30 1.3x 384 Chunks
Metropolis 50 1.5x 448 Chunks

Land

Towns can claim up to the amount of chunks listed for their level. If they need more land, they can purchase Bonus Land Chunks. Bonus Land Chunks trade off getting more land, at the cost of a higher daily upkeep. You can purchase Bonus Land Chunks up to the current level's cap. Example: Village can purchase up to 96 bonus land chunks, while Settlement can only buy 16.

Upkeep & Taxes

Towns require a Daily Upkeep to stay alive. Every new day the daily upkeep is taken from the town bank. If a town can't afford the daily upkeep, it's deleted and the land is unclaimed.

In order to help pay for the Daily Upkeep, towns can impose 3 different taxes on residents

Residency Tax

A base price that every resident of the town (except the Mayor) must pay. This amount doesn't change unless the Mayor does it.

Plot Tax

A plot tax is paid for every plot you own within a town. If a plot tax was 30 Coins, and you own 4 Plots, you would owe 120 Coins* plus any Residency Tax chosen by the Mayor.

Sales Tax (Only available with PopulaceMarket)

Sales Tax is not paid by residents, but by merchants. When an item is sold to a player, or bought from a player in Town, a percentage of the profit is taken as a sales tax. For instance, a Diamond Sword is sold for 100 Coins and the town has a 4% sales tax. The merchant would receive 96 Coins from the sale, and the town bank would receive 4 Coins.

If a resident cannot pay their taxes they are kicked from the town.

Daily Upkeep

Daily upkeep is calculated after taxes are collected from residents. Please note, when a town is created they are given a grace period. During this time, no daily upkeep is required of them.

  • For every claimed chunk, a cost is assessed. This varies based off the plot type, and the server you're playing on.
  • For every Resident in your town, a cost is assessed. The higher the rank of the resident, the more expensive.
  • For every 4 residents in your town, land cost is multiplied by +0.3%.
  • For every Bonus Land Chunk owned by your town, the Bonus Land cost price is assessed

All those factors are added up to get your Daily Upkeep, then multiplied by the Town Level Upkeep Modifier. The Daily Upkeep can be seen at any time (as well as when it's next due) from the Economy tab of your /town menu.

What are the commands?

Command Description Permission Town Rank
/allow (Player) Adds a player to the allow list for the plot you're standing in, giving them permission to bypass rank restrictions populace.commands.allow Plot Owner
/claim (Radius) Claims land for your town, or purchases a plot up for sale from the town populace.commands.claim Town Assistant/Resident
/forsale (Price) Puts the plot you're standing in up for sale populace.commands.forsale Town Assistant
/giveplot (Player) Invites a player to take ownership of the plot you're in populace.commands.giveplot Town Assistant
/invite (Player) Invites a player to town populace.commands.invite Town Manager
/jail (Player) (Time) Sends a player to the town jail populace.commands.jail Town Assistant
/join (Town) Joins a town, if you were invited or the town is open populace.commands.join --
/map Gives you a map of your town populace.commands.map Resident
/newTown (Name) Creates a new town populace.commands.newtown --
/notforsale Takes a plot of the market populace.commands.notforsale Town Assistant
/plot Shows info about the plot you're standing in populace.commands.plot --
/populace Shows plugin information and loaded modules -- --
/resident (Player) Shows resident information populace.commands.resident --
/setjail Sets the town jail to your location populace.commands.setjail Town Mayor
/town (Town) Shows info about a town populace.commands.town --
/towns Shows a list of towns on the server populace.commands.towns --
/unclaim Gives up your ownership of a plot, or unclaims land for the town populace.commands.unclaim Town Assistant/Plot Owner
/unjail (Player) Frees a player from jail populace.commands.unjail Town Assistant
/visit (Town) Warps you to the town spawn, if you have permission populace.commands.visit --
