What is Populace?

Populace is a town management plugin that works with MelonEco. I felt as though Towny was not only outdated, but difficult for new users to grasp, and this Populace was born.

What's different?

Populace is driven mostly by a powerful ingame GUI. While commands are used for simple functions, most of the information and interaction is done through the interface.

What does it do?

As of now, Populace can:

  • Allow your users to create towns, claim land, delegate permissions, and invite residents
  • Provides a great anti-grief solution that protects against all known forms of griefing
  • Creates thriving economies in survival servers
  • Allows creation of unique shops using Ender Chests and an ingame UI (Requires PopulaceMarket)
  • Allows establishment of sales tax to bring in more income for town mayors (Requires PopulaceMarket)
  • Includes a powerful GUI for viewing town information and making management of complex tasks easy
  • Includes a simple commands for a very small learning curve for players
  • Provides a complex and intuitive chat channel system, including town specific chat (Requires PopulaceChat)
  • Includes an in-depth API (including events and direct hooking to Populace interworkings) for 3rd party developers
  • Cleans up chat by only showing join and quit messages to fellow town members (Requires PopulaceJoin)
  • Allows selling of town plots to residents
  • Jail system to allow town staff to punish naughty residents, while allowing the jailed individual to leave early by choosing to leave town
  • Much more...

What are the commands?

Command Description Permission Town Rank
/allow (Player) Adds a player to the allow list for the plot you're standing in, giving them permission to bypass rank restrictions populace.commands.allow Plot Owner
/claim (Radius) Claims land for your town, or purchases a plot up for sale from the town populace.commands.claim Town Assistant/Resident
/forsale (Price) Puts the plot you're standing in up for sale populace.commands.forsale Town Assistant
/giveplot (Player) Invites a player to take ownership of the plot you're in populace.commands.giveplot Town Assistant
/invite (Player) Invites a player to town populace.commands.invite Town Manager
/jail (Player) (Time) Sends a player to the town jail populace.commands.jail Town Assistant
/join (Town) Joins a town, if you were invited or the town is open populace.commands.join --
/map Gives you a map of your town populace.commands.map Resident
/newTown (Name) Creates a new town populace.commands.newtown --
/notforsale Takes a plot of the market populace.commands.notforsale Town Assistant
/plot Shows info about the plot you're standing in populace.commands.plot --
/populace Shows plugin information and loaded modules -- --
/resident (Player) Shows resident information populace.commands.resident --
/setjail Sets the town jail to your location populace.commands.setjail Town Mayor
/town (Town) Shows info about a town populace.commands.town --
/towns Shows a list of towns on the server populace.commands.towns --
/unclaim Gives up your ownership of a plot, or unclaims land for the town populace.commands.unclaim Town Assistant/Plot Owner
/unjail (Player) Frees a player from jail populace.commands.unjail Town Assistant
/visit (Town) Warps you to the town spawn, if you have permission populace.commands.visit --
/visualize Shows a client-side border around the current plot populace.commands.visualize --
